About Deepfake Images and Videos

Deepfake technology uses artificial intelligence to create hyper-realistic fake images and videos. These manipulated media can be used for various purposes, including entertainment, but they can also pose serious ethical and security concerns.

Our Deepfake Detector helps you identify whether an image or video is real or AI-generated, providing you with a powerful tool to combat the spread of misinformation and ensure the authenticity of digital media.
